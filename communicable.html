Communicable Diseases

Communicable diseases are caused by infectious agents and can spread from one person to another through air, water, or contact.

List of Common Communicable Diseases

Disease Symptoms Prevention
Malaria Fever, chills, sweating Use mosquito nets, avoid stagnant water
Dengue High fever, joint pain, rash Prevent mosquito bites, maintain cleanliness
COVID-19 Cough, fever, shortness of breath Wear masks, hand hygiene, vaccination
Typhoid Fever, weakness, abdominal pain Boiled water, proper sanitation
Tuberculosis (TB) Chronic cough, weight loss, fatigue BCG vaccination, early diagnosis
Cholera Severe diarrhea, dehydration Safe drinking water, hygiene
