About HealthAware

HealthAware is an educational project aimed at raising awareness about common communicable and non-communicable diseases. The goal is to help people understand the symptoms, causes, and preventive measures to stay healthy.

This project is created by the following team members:

  • B. Siva Naveen
  • P. Monisha
  • K. Anusha
  • K. Harsha Vardhan

We hope this project helps spread knowledge about maintaining good health and preventing diseases in our community.
