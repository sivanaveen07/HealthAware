Non-Communicable Diseases

Non-communicable diseases (NCDs) are not transmitted from one person to another. They usually result from genetic, lifestyle, or environmental factors.

List of Common Non-Communicable Diseases

Disease Symptoms Prevention
Diabetes Fatigue, frequent urination, thirst Healthy diet, regular exercise, weight control
Hypertension (High BP) Headache, dizziness, blurred vision Low-salt diet, stress control, regular checkups
Heart Disease Chest pain, shortness of breath Exercise, avoid smoking, balanced diet
Cancer Unusual lumps, fatigue, weight loss Regular screening, avoid tobacco & alcohol
Asthma Breathing difficulty, coughing Avoid dust & allergens, use inhalers
Obesity Excess body fat, tiredness Physical activity, healthy eating habits
